BRIEF HISTORY &
ABOUT US

Royal Chapel is a contemporary Bible-believing church with its headquarters in Kumasi, Ghana, where the Word of God is taught with clarity and power in a wonderful atmosphere of worship. The mission of Gracefields is to reach out to the world with love, train Christians for excellence and raise an end-time army for the Lord. The ministry is currently headquartered at Ahodwo Nyhieso in Kumasi with several branches within the city of Kumasi, as well as other branches in Accra and in the United Kingdom
